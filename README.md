**Cyber Safe Company** is a 2D management *Serious Game* developed as a university project for the **University of Bari Aldo Moro** (A.Y. 2025-2026). The game combines strategic dynamics and role-playing (Decision Making) to teach cybersecurity management within a corporate context.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview

In *Cyber Safe Company*, players assume the role of the owner of a modern smart device (IoT) manufacturing company. The objective is not only to increase revenue but to find the perfect balance between industrial expansion and the digital defenses of the infrastructure.

### 🎯 Target Audience
* **Students (High School and University):** To acquire basic IT skills and understand the importance of security.
* **Entrepreneurs and Managers:** To learn balanced business management and realize that cybersecurity is a survival requirement, not an optional IT cost.

---

## ⚙️ Game Mechanics

The gameplay is based on a continuous economic loop of production, sale, and upgrade:

⚙️ Produce Devices ➔ 💰 Sell and Earn Coins ➔ 🛡️ Reinvest in Infrastructure and Security

1. **Production:** The company produces four lines of smart devices:
   * 🔌 Smart Plugs
   * 💡 Smart Light Bulbs
   * 📷 Smart Cameras
   * 🔒 Smart Locks
2. **Resource Management (Balance Bar):** Every upgrade shifts a balance bar. To keep the company healthy and competitive, the player must maintain the indicator within the **green zone**.
3. **Threats and Cyberattacks:** As the company grows, so does the risk. Random cyberattacks can strike at any time. If defenses are not ready, the economic consequences will be severe (e.g., an immediate loss of **30% of current funds**).

---

## 🏢 Corporate Areas (Cyber Safe Rooms)

The game is divided into several operational and control areas, each fundamental to the company's workflow:

| Room | Main Function |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Machine Room** | Area dedicated to the assembly line and the production of smart devices. |
| **Warehouse** | The warehouse where products ready for sale are stored. |
| **Server Room** | The technological heart hosting the company's network infrastructure. |
| **SIEM Room** | Control center for log monitoring and early threat detection. |
| **R&D Room** | Research and Development area to unlock new technologies. |
| **Offices** | Administrative and operational management of personnel. |

---

## 🏆 Victory Conditions

The game concludes once **all available upgrades** are completed. At the end of the match, a final feedback report will evaluate overall strategic performance based on how consistently the player maintained corporate balance.
